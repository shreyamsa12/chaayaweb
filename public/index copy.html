How It Works

For Event Organizers

  1. Sign up on Photoshooto and create an event
  2. Upload the event photos to our secure platform
  3. Share the event QR code with participants

For Participants

  1. Download the Photoshooto app
  2. Scan the event's QR code at the venue
  3. Take a selfie and let AI match your photos

For Photographers

  1. Create a profile and upload your portfolio
  2. Browse job opportunities or rent/sell equipment
  3. Get hired by agencies or directly by clients
